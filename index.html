Illustration: Marine Penvern

Our innovative company specializes in distributing custom coffee cup sleeves that showcase and promote local artists within the cozy ambiance of local coffee shops and cafes. The trajectory of the coffee market continues to ascend, fueled by expanding consumption in emerging markets, the relentless pursuit of premium experiences, and the soaring demand for organic and fairly traded products.

We're combining the evergreen appeal of coffee with the magnetic allure of local artistry--all while promoting local artists and their works. Join us as we revolutionize advertising for art, branding, and creating a visual feast for the eyes and a stimulating experience for coffee lovers everywhere.
